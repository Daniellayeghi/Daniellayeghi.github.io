Daniel Layeghi

CV · Github · Google Scholar · Past

I am currently co-founder at Z2-labs, building Souida, an API-first discovery layer that turns natural-language book queries into structured real-time retrieval for independent booksellers. For an up-to-date CV, see here. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher and completed a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Edinburgh. My PhD thesis was titled "Optimal Control Theoretic Value Function Learning." Before academia, I was an early engineer at Automata Technologies and implemented and deployed a production-grade robotics stack from scratch.
