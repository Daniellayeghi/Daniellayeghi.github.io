Problem Statement

Typical tasks in robotics.

Tasks and Objective Landscapes

  • Generally, objective landscapes are flat.
  • Not suitable for optimisation.

General Approaches

  • OC: DDP, iLQR, collocation; efficient near optima under strict objective constraints.
  • Sampling OC: MPPI, CEM, RS; flexible objective with sampling cost.
  • RL: global and general, flexible objective, inherent exploration, slower convergence.

Segment: Generalisation + Efficiency

Problem Statement

Numerical optimal control parameterises trajectories to provide locally optimal solutions under smooth differentiable objectives, while RL parameterises function spaces to synthesise approximate global policies.

Aim: combine RL-style generalisation with OC-style efficiency through a formulation that uses differentiation while learning in function space.
