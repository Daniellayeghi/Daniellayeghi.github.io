Daniel Layeghi

Daniel Layeghi is a PhD student in autonomous systems and a member of Mistry Robitics Lab and Edinburgh Centre for Robotics. His primary area of interest is in combining trajectory optimisation with learning based control to discover dynamic behaviours.
